My KodeGo Journey

I was a chat support representative, a freelancer and a full-time dad before joining KodeGo bootcamp. I discovered the bootcamp through YouTube ads and saw it as a chance for a new career. Despite having no prior coding knowledge, I eagerly learned from our teacher and engaged in self-study. My motivation was to provide a better future for my family, especially my children. I acknowledged the seriousness of coding and appreciated the opportunities and learnings provided by KodeGo. I am approaching the end of the program with confidence in my ability to achieve my dream career, thanks to KodeGo's support in connecting them with potential employers. I consider my journey with KodeGo a significant milestone in my life.
